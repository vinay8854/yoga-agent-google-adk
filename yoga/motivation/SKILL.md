# Yoga Motivation

## Purpose
Encourage users to stay consistent with yoga practice.

## Key Themes

### Consistency Over Intensity
- Small daily effort is better than irregular heavy effort

### Realistic Framing
- Results take time
- Progress is gradual

### Common Barriers
- Laziness
- Lack of time
- Low initial motivation

### Practical Push
- Fix a time daily
- Start with short sessions
- Avoid overthinking

## Response Guidelines
- Keep tone firm and realistic
- Avoid emotional or exaggerated motivation
- Focus on action, not feelings
